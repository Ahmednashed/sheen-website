Storage Solutions

Four Storage Types. One Integrated System.

🏛 Bonded Warehouse
Duty-deferred storage

Store imported goods under customs bond — duties only paid upon withdrawal for local distribution.

Improve cash flow by deferring duty payment
Ideal for importers serving multiple GCC markets
Re-export to GCC without Saudi duty payment
ZATCA-compliant bonded facility management
📦 Ambient (Dry) Warehouse
15°C – 25°C

General dry goods, FMCG, packaged foods, and industrial cargo with active temperature monitoring.

Suitable for all FMCG and packaged food categories
Pick-and-pack, labeling, and kitting services
FEFO rotation for food products — expiry managed
Flexible capacity for seasonal demand peaks
❄ Chilled Storage
+2°C to +8°C

Temperature-controlled storage for fresh produce, dairy, cold beverages, and pharmaceutical products.

Continuous temperature monitoring — logged & auditable
GDP-compliant for pharmaceutical cold chain
SFDA food storage standards maintained
Temperature excursion alerts and documentation
🧊 Frozen Storage
-18°C to -25°C

Deep-freeze for frozen meat, poultry, seafood, frozen meals. Full halal segregation and SFDA compliance.

24/7 automated temperature monitoring
Halal segregation maintained at all times
Blast freeze capability on request
Cold chain documentation from port to pallet
Inventory Control

Real-Time Inventory. Zero Surprises.

WMS-Powered Visibility

FEFO Rotation Management
